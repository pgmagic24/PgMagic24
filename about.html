About PGMagic24

Welcome to PGMagic24

PGMagic24 is a membership-based platform that enables individuals to participate in promotional programs, community activities, and lucky draw events through a simple registration process.

Both Owners and Tenants can register on the platform and receive a unique Unicode Number after successful registration and payment.

Registered members may participate in promotional campaigns, festival events, and lucky draw programs conducted by PGMagic24.

Our mission is to build a trusted and transparent membership platform that provides exciting opportunities and fair participation for all registered members across India.

PGMagic24 continuously works to improve member experience through secure registrations, transparent processes, and community-focused programs.
