Refund Policy

Registration Fees

Registration fees paid for membership and participation are generally non-refundable once the registration process has been successfully completed.

Duplicate Payments

In the event of a verified duplicate payment due to a technical issue, members may contact support for review and resolution.

Failed Transactions

If a payment is deducted but registration is not completed due to a technical issue, the matter will be reviewed and resolved according to the payment gateway and banking process.

Contact For Support

For refund-related concerns, members may contact PGMagic24 support with transaction details and payment proof.
